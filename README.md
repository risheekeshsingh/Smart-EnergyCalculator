🧠 Project Vision

The Smart Energy Calculator is built to solve a real-world problem — helping users understand and optimize their electricity usage.

In today's world, where energy consumption is rising rapidly, this tool provides:

* 📊 Transparency in electricity usage
* 💡 Awareness of appliance consumption
* 🌱 Smart suggestions to reduce bills
* ⚡ A modern digital experience

---

## 🚀 Key Highlights

* ⚡ Real-time energy calculation
* 💰 Accurate electricity bill estimation
* 📊 Appliance-level breakdown
* 🧠 Smart energy-saving recommendations
* 🎨 Premium UI with animations
* 🎥 Video background login experience
* 🌙 Clean, modern design (Glassmorphism inspired)


 ✨ Features (Detailed)

🔹 1. Energy Consumption Calculator

* Input:

  * Appliance name
  * Power rating (Watts)
  * Usage hours per day

* Output:

  * Daily consumption (kWh)
  * Monthly consumption
  * Estimated electricity cost

---

🔹 2. Electricity Bill Estimator

* Calculates total bill based on:

  * Units consumed
  * Custom electricity rate

* Instant results with dynamic updates

🔹 3. Appliance-wise Breakdown

* Shows detailed energy usage per appliance
* Helps identify:

  * High consumption devices
  * Optimization opportunities

🔹 4. Smart Energy Suggestions

* Suggests:

  * Reducing usage time
  * Switching to efficient appliances
  * Better consumption habits

🔹 5. Bill Breakdown Visualization

* Animated output of:

  * Total units
  * Total cost
  * Contribution of each appliance

🔹 6. Solar Panel Recommendation (Advanced)

* Suggests solar adoption based on usage
* Helps users:

  * Reduce dependency on grid
  * Move towards sustainable energy

🔹 7. Modern UI/UX Experience

* 🎨 Glassmorphism design
* 💡 Smooth animations
* 🎥 Video background login page
* 🧩 Clean layout and responsive design

⚙️ Working Principle

📌 Step-by-Step Flow

1. User enters appliance data

2. System calculates:
   Energy (kWh) = (Power × Hours × Days) / 1000


3. Then computes:
   Bill = Energy × Cost per Unit
4. Displays:

   * Total consumption ⚡
   * Total bill 💰
   * Appliance breakdown 📊
   * Suggestions 🌱
